The Levitz Brace

Dr. Martin Jungmann did extensive research in the 1930's and the effects of gravitational pull on posture and how that related to chronic back pain. He developed this concept that changes the postural alignment of the spine as it relates to the sacrum and pelvis, thus relieving pain. The device is specifically designed to correct sagittal plane decompensation.



The pads, when properly fitted, rest against the patient over the pubic symphysis and the sacrum.

The Levitz will apply various amounts of pressure that changes weight bearing and effects of gravity upon the spine.

The amount of pressure applied may be adjusted with a special scale that measures the amount of pressure being produced.



The Levitz uses Dr. Jungmann's technique, and the newly designed pads allow the patient a better range of motion and better flexibility.
