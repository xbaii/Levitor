The Levitz Brace

Dr. Martin Jungmann did extensive research on chronic back pain in the 1930's and the effects of gravitational pull on posture and how that related to chronic back pain. He developed this concept that changes the postural alignment of the spine as it relates to the sacrum and pelvis, thus relieving pain.



Specifically designed to correct sagittal plane decompensation.

The pads, when properly fitted, rest against the patient over the pubic symphysis and the sacrum.

The Levitz will produce a pressure in the sagittal plane of 6.5 to 7.5 pounds.

The device changes weight bearing and effects of gravity upon the spine.
